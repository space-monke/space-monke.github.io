What is KKKTD?

KKKTD is a revolutionary game, designed and programmed by monke, and partially voiced by lord qiro (without consent).

KKKTD is an anagram of ku klux klan tower defense, the game features a diverse list of towers consisting of:

Regular klansmen

Imperial wizards

And the grand wizards.

The enemies being just as diverse, consist of:

Regular coons

Watermelon niggaz

And the to be added giganigga
